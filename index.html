What

In a world where Web Developers and UX/UI Designers co-exist to turn the digital realm into a better place, you will find your mission by creating a landing page for something crazy-amazing!

When

This Friday

Jan 18th

How

UX/UI Designers and Web Developers will trade places for 1h30!

  1. After introducing the Web Dev class to Wireframing, they will have 20 minutes to draw one low-fidelity screen of what they want to make for their final project;
  2. After that, they will give you their screen and you have to write the HTML for it. You'll have 40 minutes;
  3. Finally, everyone will have 30 minutes to comment: Web Developers will tell you how you could improve your code; UX/UI Designers will tell you how you can improve your wireframes!
